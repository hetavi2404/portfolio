About Me

I’m a second-year Computer Engineering student with a CGPA of 7.71, currently exploring the real world side of what we’re taught in class. I’ve worked with C, C++, HTML, and CSS through hands-on learning at Royal Technosoft. Right now, I’m focused on strengthening my skills in Python, JavaScript, SQL, and Data Structures & Algorithms.

I enjoy the kind of programming that actually makes you think — not just follow instructions. I prefer solving logical problems, experimenting with code, and understanding the "why" behind what works. While I’m not obsessed with frontend aesthetics, I do believe clean structure and clarity matter — in both code and presentation.

I'm not rushing to chase trends or pretend I know it all. I’m building my foundation the way I like to build my code — step by step, with intention. This portfolio is one of those steps.
